CRITICAL SERVICE

Vijay Hospital Critical Care Expertise

Vijay hospital is one of the best critical care hospital in Chittor.Vijay hospital have a specialization of doctors who deals specifically with patients experiencing high-dependency, life threatening conditions. There are a range of ICU specialties where critical care doctors work including surgical, trauma, medical and high risk conditions. Critical care can be mentally and physically demanding with long hours (including night shifts), extensive patient handling and high-pressure conditions. Critical care nurses must be able to make sophisticated judgments quickly as patients in ICU are often unstable and can experience rapid physiological decline.
